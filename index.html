About me

I’m Malik Nielsen, a voice actor working in both English and Danish. I’ve spent years building experience through character work, fandubs, and creative voice projects.

My main focus is character performance — emotional, grounded, or comedic — with clean, consistent audio. I like working on projects where the voice helps tell the story, not just read lines.

If you want a voice that feels natural, clear, and committed to the scene, I’d love to hear about your project.

  • English & Danish voice work
  • Character acting (serious + comedic)
  • Clean recording + careful editing
