About me

My name is Malik Nielsen, and I work as a voice actor in both English and Danish. I provide voices for a range of characters and other types of projects.

I started working with voice acting in 2022 under a different name. What began as a hobby through my own projects on YouTube and collaborations with others has since grown into something I approach more professionally.

I work with the types of projects listed on this site — including games, animation, dubbing, audiobooks, and commercials — and I’m also open to other kinds of work. When recording a role, I focus on following the director’s instructions and making sure the character fits the scene and project as intended.

If you’re looking for a voice for a project and want to work with someone who takes the job seriously, feel free to get in touch.
